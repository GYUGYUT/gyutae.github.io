Patent

Device and Method for Diagnosing Exposure Keratopathy based on Multimodal Fusion Learning
Application No. 10-2025-0121342 · Filed Aug 28, 2025
Two-stage multimodal fusion learning framework for automatic grading of Exposure Keratopathy (EK). Trained with four ocular imaging modalities collected at a tertiary hospital (broadbeam, slit-beam, scattering, and blue-light): modality-wise features are aligned and fused during training, while inference enables modality-agnostic classification using only a single broadbeam image to implicitly leverage information from other modalities.
